DJ App

The DJ App is a fully functional music player that allows you and anyone to:

  • Play/Pause the current song by using the middle red button under the song queue
  • Skip to the next or previous song by using the left and right red buttons
  • Go to a specific time in the current song by using the pink seeker
  • Change the current song's speed (BPM) by adjusting the blue BPM slider
  • Adjust the song queue by one of the following ways:
    • Sorting the queue by song genre using the buttons on the right in the Auto-DJ section.
    • Sorting the queue by song speed (BPM) using the buttons on the right in the Auto-DJ section.
    • Dragging & dropping songs directly from and to the queue. This allows the user to be precise and achieve the queue that they want!
  • Change the color of the lights by using the color wheel to the right of the BPM slider.
  • See the lyrics of the current song and perform karaoke by clicking the aptly named "Karaoke" button.
  • Add voice effects to your karaoke performance by clicking the "Effects" button.
  • Create a backup of the current state of the DJ Applet, that includes:
    • The state of the song queue
    • The speed (BPM) of the current song
    • The color of the lights
    • The time of the current song
    • The ratings in the karaoke window
    To create a backup, go to the top-left of the window and click the "File". After that, a dialog box saying "Create Backup" will appear, click and like magic you have created a backup of the DJ!

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add my own songs to the song queue?

Unfortunately, you cannot. The song queue contains songs from the museums featured artists and due to legal copyright reasons, we cannot allow users to play their own songs.

Do I have to manually play the next song after the current one finishes playing?

No, after the current song finishes playing the DJ will auto-play the next one!

Why is the "Effects" button greyed out and why can't I click it?

For you to be able to click the "Effects" button, you must have the Karaoke window open!

Karaoke

The karaoke window provides the user with the lyrics to the current song and also gives everyone else in the room the ability to rate the singer's perfomance!.

If you are the singer, do not worry about having to change the lyrics once the song changes, that happens automatically!

If you are in the crowd, you can rate the singer by using the purple buttons labelled 1 through 5! Be a good sport though, it's hard being on stage!

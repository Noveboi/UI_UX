Viewing Room

The 'landing page'. There are two main elements, the 3 buttons and the red button at the top-right.

  • By clicking one of the 3 buttons, you will be taken to the corresponding artist's personal hall, where you can find various videos featuring said artist.
  • The red button takes you to the cafeteria, where you can order coffee, food, drinks & more.

Personal Artist Hall

Each artist's hall has the following videos:

  • 2 videos taken from a concert of theirs.
  • 1 video featuring an interview with the artist.
  • 1 video featuring a cover of an artist song.

By clicking any one of the icons, you will be taken to the corresponding YouTube video.

Cafeteria

In the cafeteria, you can directly receive help from our staff by clicking the "Got a question?" button!

Upcoming Concerts

The "landing page" of this applet. Besides the information in the top of the page, you can find 3 buttons, each containing the following information:

  • The artist's name
  • The date and time the concert will take place
  • The price range per ticket. (€ = low price | €€€ = high price)

If you are interested in a concert listed here, click the button with the artist that you want. You will then be transferred to the Ticket Purchase page, where you get to choose the location

Ticket Purchase

After you choose the concert you want to check out, you are transferred here. You are greeted with two sections:

  • Concert Hall Map

    This map is an overhead view of the concert hall, located in the Museum's premises. You can choose to be in one of the following areas:

    • General Admission (€)

      The cheapest and most 'common' area. You will still have a good view of the stage, just at a slight distance.

    • VIP (€€€)

      The most expensive and 'exclusive' area. You will be right next to the stage, without obstructions. You will feel the sound!

    • Side VIP (€€)

      These areas offer a side view of the stage. You will be very close to the stage, but the sound will not be as it is in the VIP section.

    There are also different colors that signify to the user the following:

    • Red/Brown Area

      An area you can select

    • Blue Area

      The area you have currently selected

    • Grey Area

      This area is the stage, it always shows the artist's name and cannot be selected

  • Price & Ticket Amount

    Here you can see a live price indication as well as the quantity of tickets that you have selected. By default, the ticket quantity is 1, but you can change that by using the red and green button right beside the ticket number or by clicking in the "number box" and typing in how many tickets you want. (A valid number of tickets is between 1 and 99)
